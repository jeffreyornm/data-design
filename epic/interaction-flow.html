Use Case:

Title: Creating a room so people can join to listen to music.

Description: John wants to create a room so that people can join him and listen to music together on Plug.dj.

Name of User and their role: John; music enthusiast who enjoys listening to music with others and experiencing music he hasn't heard of before.

Usage Preconditions: Must have a Plug.dj account and be logged into the site.

Usage Postconditions: Created a room and is now listening to music with other people.

Interaction Flow:

  • John enters Plug.dj.com into a browser of his choice.
  • The server returns with the Plug.dj homepage.
  • John will sign into his account.
  • Server takes you to your account.
  • John wil click on the avatar icon.
  • Options pop down.
  • John will select the settings cogwheel icon.
  • Server responds by opening a new page with account information.
  • My communities (room) will appear second from the bottom right-hand corner of the page.
  • John will click on the my communities button.
  • Server responds by bringing up a create community page.
  • John now has the option to name his community(room).
  • John can also set his room to public or private.
  • John will select create community.
  • Server responds by displaying John's new room along with the url to his newly created room.
  • John is now in his newly created room playing music.

Frequency of Use: 4-5 times a week.
